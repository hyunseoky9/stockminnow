About Stockminnow

Stockminnow is an advanced decision-support platform designed to optimize fish hatchery management strategies for endangered species conservation. Built on cutting-edge reinforcement learning algorithms, it simulates complex population dynamics and genetic factors to help researchers make informed stocking decisions.

This interactive environment allows researchers to explore various management scenarios, compare human decision-making with AI-driven policies, and contribute valuable data to conservation research efforts. Each simulation session provides insights into population genetics, habitat management, and long-term species viability.
